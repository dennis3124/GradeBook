How do I add a semester?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the plus button at the bottom right. A pop up window will appear. Enter the Semester name and year in the textboxes. You can choose it to be your current semester by clicking the checkbox at the bottom. Finally, click the submit button. The semester you added will appear on the page.

How do I add a course?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the semester you want the course to be added to. Click the plus button at the bottom right. A pop up window will appear. Enter the course Name and ID in the textboxes. Choose the credit hours from the drop down menu. Finally, click the submit button. The course you added will appear on the page.

How do I add Homework/quiz/exam section to a course?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the semester where the course is. After that, click the course. Click the plus button at the bottom right. A pop up window will appear. Enter the section name and weight in the textboxes. Finally, click the submit button. The section you added will appear on the page.

How do I add a grade for a section?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the semester where the course is. After that, click the course. Next, Click the section tab you want to add a grade to. Click the orange plus button. Enter the grade name, grade received and grade total. Finally click the tick button. The grade name will appear on the page.

How do I delete a semester?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the semester you want to delete. Finally, click the rubbish bin button. The semester would be deleted.

How do I delete a course?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the semester you want the course to be deleted from. Finally, click the rubbish bin button. The course would be deleted.

How do I delete/edit a grade from a section?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the semester where the course is. After that, click the course. Next, Click the section tab you want to remove a grade from. Click the 3 dot button and choose whether to edit or delete the grade. Finally click the option. The grade name would be removed or edited.

How do I check my GPA?

Click the GPA button on the task bar. Your GPA for each semester and cumulative GPA would be displayed there.

How do I calculate/estimate my grade for a course?

Click the Grade Calculator button on the task bar. Then, click the course you want to calculate/estimate the grade. A pop up window will appear showing the grade break down of the course and the estimated grade. These are already calculated. To estimate your grade based on the final, enter the letter grade you want and worth of the final in the textboxes and press the estimate button. The amount of marks needed to attain that grade would be shown.

How do I edit my profile?

Click your name on the top right corner. On the page displayed, you can upload a profile picture, add/edit your name and email. Press submit to save the changes.

How do I logout?

Click the logout button on the taskbar. You would be logged out.

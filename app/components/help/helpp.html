How do I add a semester?

Click the Semesters button on the taskbar. Then, click the plus button at the bottom right. A pop up window will appear. Enter the Semester name and year in the textboxes. You can choose it to be your current semester by clicking the checkbox at the bottom. Finally, click the submit button. The semester you added will appear on the page.
